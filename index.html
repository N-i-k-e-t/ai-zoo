Why This Exists

AI is powerful.
But hard to see.

Most people interact with AI through chat boxes that hide its behavioral nature. AI Zoo changes that — it makes agents observable.

Observe
Watch agents act in real time — scanning, foraging, deciding, hesitating, failing, retrying. See behavior, not just output.
Understand
Every species has a class, habitat, diet, strengths, and limits. Build real mental models — not hype, not fear.
Interact
Feed agents tasks. Toggle stimuli. Watch how they react. Always safe. Always sandboxed. Always revealing.
Compare
Different species. Different speeds, caution, creativity, and risk profiles. See the real differences clearly.
